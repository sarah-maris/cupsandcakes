To be noted, a cleanse is not a diet. Like all muscles, when given a rest, in this case from processed or heavy foods, our digestive organs can return to function more efficiently. A cleanse is an opportunity to give your body a break while supplying it with nutrient-rich hydration. It’s an opportunity to step away from old habits and “fixes,” clean your soul-house and rejuvenate. Spinach Salad with Lemon-Oil Dressing

Salad 1 cup spinach, torn to bite size pieces 1/2 avocado, chopped 1/3 red onion, sliced thin 8 almonds, sliced or chopped 1 med carrot grated 1 cup sprouts 1/2 cup fresh parsley 1 med tomato diced

Dressing Juice of 1 Lemon 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil 1 teaspoon dried oregano 1/4 teaspoon black pepper Dash of Sea Salt to taste
