Cups & Cakes is pleased to offer gourmet retail stores and restaurants the opportunity to add a broad selection of locally-made, exquisite and scrumptious cakes and desserts to their menus.
Cups & Cakes provides desserts to many of the  businesses in the Two River area, including The Atlantic Club, The Navesink Fishery, Jersey Shore Coffee Roasters, Mama Bears Play Cafe and Woody’s.  Our prompt customer service and dependably delicious products will make your dessert purchases easy as pie (or cake)!Our ordering process is simple and easy:
  • We establish a convenient day (Monday-Friday), each week, for your deliveries or order pick-ups.
  •  We suggest that you assign one or two staff members to be the point person(s) responsible for communicating your dessert orders to ensure consistent communication regarding your inventory needs.
  • Contact Denise to begin the process of setting up an account or to get wholesale pricing for our products.
  • Delivery orders require a minimum of 48-working hours advanced notice of the delivery date.
  • We require a minimum order of $75.00, within the local area, to qualify for free delivery.
  • During holiday or other peak times, orders will be processed in the order taken. Call or contact us early!
  • All payments are COD. Credit card can be kept on file to set up automatic payment.
